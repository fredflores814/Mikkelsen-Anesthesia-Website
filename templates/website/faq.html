Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our mobile anesthesia services

Mobile anesthesia involves bringing hospital-grade anesthesia services directly to dental, oral surgery, or medical offices. I provide safe, in-office sedation and anesthesia, eliminating the need for a hospital or surgery center visit while maintaining the same standards of care.

I provide a full range of anesthesia options, including:

  • Local anesthesia (in coordination with your provider)
  • Monitored Anesthesia Care and IV Sedation
  • Regional Anesthesia
  • General anesthesia

The type of anesthesia used depends on the procedure, your medical history, comfort level, and collaboration between your provider and anesthesia team.

Office-based anesthesia is safe when delivered by experienced anesthesia professionals using hospital-grade equipment and monitoring. I am a board-certified registered nurse anesthetists (CRNA) trained in outpatient and office-based settings. Patients are carefully evaluated beforehand and continuously monitored before, during, and after the procedure.

I follow nationally recognized standards for office-based anesthesia care, monitoring, and emergency preparedness.

Your procedure will take place in your provider's office or a designated outpatient setting. I bring all necessary anesthesia equipment, medications, and monitoring tools directly to the location.

Local Anesthesia

Local anesthesia numbs a small, specific area — such as a tooth or section of skin — so treatment can be completed comfortably while the patient remains awake.

MAC & IV Sedation

MAC and IV sedation combine pain management with relaxation, allowing patients to stay comfortable while breathing on their own. Ideal for outpatient procedures performed in-office.

Regional Anesthesia

Regional anesthesia numbs a specific part of the body, such as an arm or leg, while patients remain awake and comfortable. It provides excellent pain control for longer procedures and is easily performed in a mobile, office-based setting.

General Anesthesia

General anesthesia provides complete unconsciousness and pain control for complex or lengthy procedures. Delivered through IV or inhalation, it's safely managed in your office with full monitoring and recovery oversight.

Pediatric Anesthesia

Children require specialized care when it comes to anesthesia. Our pediatric anesthesia services are designed to keep young patients safe, comfortable, and calm during dental or outpatient procedures. With experienced CRNAs, hospital-grade monitoring, and family-focused communication, we deliver trusted in-office anesthesia care for children across Wisconsin and Northern Illinois.

Specialized Care Services

High-Anxiety & Trauma-Informed Anesthesia Care

Patients with severe dental anxiety, prior procedural trauma, or difficulty tolerating treatment often require structured sedation planning beyond standard approaches. I provide office-based IV sedation and general anesthesia tailored for high-anxiety patients, allowing dental and oral surgery practices to safely manage complex cases while maintaining predictable workflow and monitoring standards.

Special Needs & Neurodiverse Anesthesia Care

Patients with developmental disabilities, sensory sensitivities, or complex medical needs require individualized anesthesia planning. I partner with dental and oral surgery practices to deliver structured, office-based sedation and general anesthesia that supports safe case completion while maintaining hospital-level monitoring in a familiar clinical setting.

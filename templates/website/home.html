Experience You Can Trust

I am a Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetist with over 26 years of healthcare experience, including 5 years as a National Guard medic and 17 years as a CRNA providing anesthesia care in hospitals, ambulatory surgery centers, and office-based settings. I founded Mikkelsen Anesthesia LLC with a simple goal: to deliver safe, high-quality anesthesia care in a way that feels personal, attentive, and reassuring for every patient I serve.

Mikkelsen Anesthesia LLC is a mobile anesthesia practice. I bring comprehensive anesthesia services directly to the office or surgical setting you choose, allowing patients and providers to receive hospital-level care within the convenience of their own clinical environment.

Personalized, Start-to-Finish Care

When you work with me, you work directly with me from start to finish. I personally schedule and provide every anesthesia service, ensuring true continuity of care, full accountability, and a consistently high standard of practice.

From our very first conversation, I guide you through the pre-operative process, answer your questions, and help you feel fully prepared. I remain by your side throughout your procedure and continue to be available afterward to support you during recovery.

Uncompromising Safety Standards

Safety is always my highest priority. Office-based anesthesia must meet rigorous safety expectations, and I take that responsibility seriously. During your procedure, you are continuously monitored for oxygen saturation, heart rate, blood pressure, and ventilation.

I bring industry-leading anesthesia equipment, advanced monitoring technology, and full emergency medications and equipment to every location to ensure the highest level of preparedness and protection.

Compassionate, Individualized Approach

I have extensive experience caring for patients who experience anxiety, as well as pediatric, special needs, and neurodiverse patients. I understand that anesthesia can feel intimidating, and I believe feeling heard and understood is just as important as clinical expertise.

I take the time to listen, tailor my approach to your individual needs, and create a calm, reassuring environment where you can feel confident and at ease.

Collaborative, Concierge-Level Service

I partner closely with surgeons and facility teams to provide seamless, concierge-level anesthesia services tailored to each practice. Above all, my focus remains on you — your safety, your comfort, and your peace of mind. Contact me for a consultation or questions.

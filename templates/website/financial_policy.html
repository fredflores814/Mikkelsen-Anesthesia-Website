Financial Policy & Payment

Transparent pricing and flexible payment options for your anesthesia care

Mikkelsen Anesthesia LLC is dedicated to providing high-quality, office-based anesthesia services in a safe, comfortable, and efficient setting. As a primarily fee-for-service provider, I am committed to transparency regarding costs and payment expectations.

Direct Payment Policy

I operate as a cash-based provider. Full payment is required 5 to 8 days prior to the scheduled procedure unless otherwise arranged.

Accepted forms of payment include cash and major credit/debit cards. Third-party financing options may be available.

Insurance & Reimbursement

I do not bill insurance and am considered out-of-network for most plans.

Detailed statements or superbills can be provided for potential reimbursement.

Cost Estimates

Fee estimates are provided in advance and include pre-op assessment, anesthesia administration, and post-op monitoring.

A $400 deposit is required within 48 hours of booking to begin preoperative preparation. This deposit will be applied toward the total cost of the procedure. Please note that the deposit is non-refundable if the procedure is canceled.

Cancellation Policy

96+ hours' notice: eligible for refund per policy
48–96 hours' notice: 50% of anesthesia fee retained
24–48 hours' notice or no-show: no refund

Refunds for eligible cancellations or overpayments are processed within 10 business days.

Have Questions About Payment?

I am here to help you understand your financial options and find the best solution for your needs.

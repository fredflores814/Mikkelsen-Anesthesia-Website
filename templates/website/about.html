About Mikkelsen Anesthesia

Excellence in mobile anesthesia services since our founding

Our Story & Mission

Mikkelsen Anesthesia was established to provide comprehensive anesthesia services specializing in mobile anesthesia solutions for office-based and outpatient healthcare settings. Our company delivers custom-tailored anesthesia services across a variety of outpatient environments.

We focus on enhancing patient experience, comfort, and safety by offering a range of anesthesia options, from local anesthesia to deep sedation and general anesthesia, depending on procedural requirements and patient needs.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional anesthesia care that ensures patient safety, comfort, and peace of mind while enabling healthcare providers to deliver optimal treatment in outpatient settings.
